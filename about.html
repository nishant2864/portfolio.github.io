About Me

AI Aficionado

Greetings!

I'm Nishant Bhardwaj, a dedicated web developer and AI enthusiast with a passion for creating innovative digital solutions. My journey in the tech world has been driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and a commitment to excellence.

My path in the digital realm has been anything but conventional. Unlike those who meticulously plan each step, I have often made decisions on the spot, guided by my instincts and immediate circumstances.

After completing my 12th grade, I was at a crossroads, torn between pursuing a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) and a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.). My ultimate dream is to clear the UPSC exams and become an IAS officer, a goal driven by my desire to establish my own identity and contribute something new to society. Initially, I leaned towards B.A., aligning with my aspirations for public service.

However, a twist of fate came with my JEE Mains results, where I scored 87 percentile on my first attempt. This unexpected success prompted me to reconsider and choose B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) over B.A. Despite my hopes, I couldn't secure a seat in IIT or NIT. I decided to join Galgotias University, determined to make the most of the opportunities there.

Just a month into my classes at Galgotias, I received an admission letter from NIT Bhopal. The course offered was not aligned with my interests, and I made a pivotal decision to stay at Galgotias University. Here, I resolved to forge my own path, refine my skills, and build my unique identity.

What excites me the most about technology is its ability to transform ideas into reality. My passion lies in developing seamless and visually appealing web experiences that not only meet but exceed user expectations. Whether it's designing elegant user interfaces or solving intricate back-end problems, I thrive on creating solutions that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

I have had the privilege of working on various projects and internships that have significantly shaped my professional journey. As an intern at AICTE Eduskills Foundations, I gained hands-on experience in intelligent automation and advanced data handling. This opportunity allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, further fueling my interest in AI and automation.

In addition, my role as the Management Lead at TechDesk 2024 provided me with invaluable leadership and organizational skills. Leading a team to organize tech events and workshops was a challenging yet rewarding experience that taught me the importance of teamwork and effective communication.
